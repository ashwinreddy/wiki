**Markov chain Monte Carlo (MCMC)** is a method for sampling from a distribution or approximating an expectation.


As this [article](https://jeremykun.com/2015/04/06/markov-chain-monte-carlo-without-all-the-bullshit/) explains,

> Markov Chain Monte Carlo is a technique to solve the problem of _sampling from a complicated distribution_.
