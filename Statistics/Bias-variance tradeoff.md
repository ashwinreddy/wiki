The **bias-variance tradeoff** is a property of a model that the [[variance]] of a parameter estimator can be reduced at the cost of increasing bias.

Suppose $Y = f(X) + \epsilon$ where $\epsilon \sim \mathcal{N}(0, \sigma_\epsilon)$

\begin{align\*}
\operatorname{Error}(x) &= \mathbb{E}\left[\left(Y - \hat{f}(x)\right)^2\right]
\end{align\*}
