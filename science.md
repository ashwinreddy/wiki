science generates [[knowledge]] and [[explanation]]s about the world through experiments designed on the basis of [[empiricism]] and mathematical models. Philosophically, it relies on pragmatism. 

[[deleuze]] says science has no need for [[philosophy]] to reflect and communicate. Science creates functives, not [[concept]]s. Even when you have a function, you still have to discover the underlying concept.
