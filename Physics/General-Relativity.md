# Special Relativity

Applies only to IRFs

\begin{equation}
\gamma = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-\frac{v^2}{c^2}}}
\end{equation}

If $v\ll{c}$, then $\gamma \approx 1$. $\gamma$ is the **Lorentz factor**.

When you start moving,

- length contracts
- time dilates
