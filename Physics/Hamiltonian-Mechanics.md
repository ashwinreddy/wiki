## Legendre Transform

if $f$ is a function of $x$ and $y$,

$$
\mathrm{d}f = \frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\mathrm{d}x + \frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\mathrm{d}y = u\,\mathrm{d}x + w\,\mathrm{d}y
$$

$$
\mathrm{d}(wy) = y\,\mathrm{d}w + w\,\mathrm{d}y
$$

$$
\mathrm{d}f - \mathrm{d}(wy) = \mathrm{d}g
$$
