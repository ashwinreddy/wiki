The **free electron model** describes the free-roaming valence electrons in the atoms of a solid. Suppose the object is a rectangular solid such that it is a 3D [[infinite square well]]

$$
V(x, y, z) = \begin{cases} 0 & (x, y,z) \in [0, \ell_x] \times [0, \ell_y] \times [0, \ell_z] \\\\ \infty & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}
$$

Then the wave function

$$
\psi_{n_x n_y n_z} = \sqrt{\frac{8}{\ell_x \ell_y \ell_z}} \sin\left(\frac{n_x \pi}{\ell_x}x\right)\sin\left(\frac{n_y \pi}{\ell_y}y\right)\sin\left(\frac{n_z \pi}{\ell_z}z\right)
$$

$$
E_{n_x n_y n_z} = \frac{\hbar^2 k^2}{2m}
$$
