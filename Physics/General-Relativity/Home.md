The theory of **General relativity** posits that the gravitational phenomenon is not simply a force, but that it is a reflection of a warped spacetime. In the words of John Wheeler,

> Matter tells spacetime how to curve. Spacetime tells matter how to move.

\end{equation}


# Geodesic Equation

\begin{equation}
\frac{\mathrm{d}^2x^\mu}{\mathrm{d}\lambda^2}+\Gamma^\mu_{\alpha\beta}\frac{\mathrm{d}x^\alpha}{\mathrm{d}\lambda}\frac{\mathrm{d}x^\beta}{\mathrm{d}\lambda} =0
\end{equation}

$$
\lambda = a\tau + b
$$
