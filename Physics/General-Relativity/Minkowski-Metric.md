The **Minkowski** metric describes flat spacetime.

\begin{equation}
\eta = \begin{bmatrix}
\mp 1 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\\
0 & \pm 1 & 0 & 0 \\\
0 & 0 & \pm 1 & 0 \\\
0 & 0 & 0 & \pm 1
\end{bmatrix}
\end{equation}

In Minkowski space,

\begin{equation}
s^2 = \eta_{\mu\nu}\Delta x^\mu \Delta x^\nu
\end{equation}
