# Gravitation Near Earth

\begin{equation}
\vec{F}_g = m\vec{g} \qquad \vec{g} = -g\mathbf{\hat{j}} \qquad g \approx 9.8\rm{\frac{m}{s^2}}
\end{equation}

# Newton's General Gravitation

\begin{equation}
\vec{g}\left(\vec{r}\right) = Gm_1\vec\nabla\left[\frac{1}{r}  \right]
\end{equation}

## Gauss's Law for Gravity

\begin{equation}
\vec\nabla\cdot\vec{g} \iff \oint{\vec{g}\cdot\diff{\vec{A}}} = -4\pi{GM}
\end{equation}
