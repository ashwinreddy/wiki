# Newton's General Gravitation

\begin{equation}
\vec{g}\left(\vec{r}\right) = Gm_1\vec\nabla\left[\frac{1}{r}  \right]
\end{equation}

## Gauss's Law for Gravity

Note similarity to [Gauss's Law for Electric Fields](Electric-Field#introduction_gauss-s-law)

\begin{equation}
\vec\nabla\cdot\vec{g} = -4\pi{G}\rho \iff \oint{\vec{g}\cdot\diff{\vec{A}}} = -4\pi{GM}
\end{equation}
