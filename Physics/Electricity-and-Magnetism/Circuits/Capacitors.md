# Introduction
A **capacitor** is a device that can store charge.

\begin{equation}
q = C V
\end{equation}

### Parallel Plate Capacitors

$$
C = \frac{\epsilon_0 A}{d}
$$

### Series and Parallel

For series,
\begin{equation}
\frac{1}{C_{eq}} = \sum_i{\frac{1}{C_i}}
\end{equation}

For parallel,
\begin{equation}
C_{eq} = \sum_i C_i
\end{equation}
