**Work** is a change in [[energy]]. The work done by net force is a change in [[kinetic energy]].

\begin{equation}
\sum_i W_i = \Delta K
\end{equation}


# Work-Energy Theorem

Proof starts with Newton's 2nd law.

\begin{gather}
\mathbf{F} =  m\mathbf{a} \\\\
\int_{\gamma} \mathbf{F} \cdot \dd{\mathbf{r}} = m \int_{t_1}^{t_2} \mathbf{a} \cdot \dv{\mathbf{r}}{t}\dd{t} \\\\
\int_{\gamma} \mathbf{F} \cdot \dd{\mathbf{r}} = m \int_{t_1}^{t_2} \left( \mathbf{a} \cdot \mathbf{v} \right) \dd{t} \\\\
\end{gather}
