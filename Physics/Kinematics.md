## Kinematics

### Rectilinear Uniform Acceleration Motion

You only need to know three equations to solve any uniform acceleration motion problem.

\begin{equation*}
\Delta{v} = at
\end{equation*}

\begin{equation*}
\Delta{x} = v_0t+\frac{1}{2}at^2
\end{equation*}

\begin{equation}
\Delta(v^2) = 2a\Delta{x}
\end{equation}
