The **Lorentz transformation** tells you how to change your coordinate system to a friend's (in a manner consistent with special relativity).

\begin{align}
x' &= \gamma(x-vt) \\\\\
t' &= \gamma(t-vx/c^2)
\end{align}


\begin{equation}
\begin{bmatrix} x' \\\\\ t' \end{bmatrix} = \gamma\begin{bmatrix} 1 & -\beta \\\\\ \beta & 1 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} x \\\\\ t \end{bmatrix}
\end{equation}
