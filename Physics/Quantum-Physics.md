## Schrodinger Equation

All information in wave function $\psi$. Operators take in functions and spit out functions (denoted with hat here). Linear operators have eigenfunctions.


$$
\partial_t\big|\psi\left(\vec{r},t\right)\big\rangle = \frac{1}{\iota\hbar }\hat{\mathcal{H}}\, \big|\psi\left(\vec{r},t\right)\big\rangle
$$

$$
\hat{H} = \frac{\hat{p}^2}{2m} + U(x)
$$

$$
\iota\hbar\dot{\psi} = \frac{-\hbar^2}{2m}\nabla^2\psi + V(x,y,z)\psi
$$
