# Gauss's Law

\begin{equation}
\vec\nabla\cdot\vec{E} = \frac{\rho}{\epsilon_0} \iff \Phi_E = \oint{\vec{E}\cdot\diff{\vec{A}}} = \frac{q_{enc}}{\epsilon_0}
\end{equation}

# Faraday's Law

\begin{equation}
\vec\nabla\times\vec{E} = -\frac{\partial \vec{B}}{\partial t} \iff \oint{\vec{E}\cdot\diff{\vec{s}}} = -\deriv{\phi_B}{t}
\end{equation}
