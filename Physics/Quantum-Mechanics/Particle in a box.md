**Particle in a box** refers to 

$$
V(x) = \begin{cases} 0 & 0 \leq x \leq a \\\\ \infty & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}
$$

Outside the well, $\psi(x) = 0$. Inside the well, $V=0$ and the [[Schrodinger equation]] says

$$
-\frac{\hbar^2}{2m} \frac{\dd{}^2 \psi}{\dd{} x^2} = E \psi
$$

More neatly,

$$
\frac{\dd{}^2 \psi}{\dd{} x^2} = -k^2 \psi, \qquad k = \frac{\sqrt{2mE}}{\hbar}
$$

This equation is exactly what we have for the [[simple harmonic motion]]
