**Spin** is a kind of internal angular momentum for elementary particles. The spin quantum number takes on values $s = 0, \frac{1}{2}, 1, \frac{3}{2}, 2, \dots$. The spin angular momentum is then

$$
S = \hbar \sqrt{s(s+1)}
$$

$$
S^2 | sm \rangle = \hbar^2 s(s+1) | sm \rangle
$$

$$
S_z | sm \rangle = \hbar m| sm \rangle
$$
