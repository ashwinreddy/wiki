The **time-independent Schrodinger equation** is a simplification of the [[Schrodinger equation]] that happens to be extremely useful in solving the original.

\begin{equation}
\label{eq:operator-tise}
\widehat{H} \psi = E \psi} \tag{T.I.S.E.
\end{equation}

\begin{equation}
-\frac{\hbar^2}{2m} \frac{\dd{}^2 \psi}{\dd{} x^2} + V\psi = E \psi
\end{equation}
