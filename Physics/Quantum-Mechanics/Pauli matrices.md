The **Pauli matrices** are [[Hermitian|Hermitian matrix]] [[matrices|matrix]]

\begin{gather}
\sigma_x = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 1 \\\\ 1 & 0 \end{bmatrix} \\\\
\sigma_y = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & -\iota \\\\ \iota & 0 \end{bmatrix} \\\\
\sigma_z = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 \\\\ 0 & -1 \end{bmatrix}
\end{gather}
