hannah arendt (1906-1975) was an influential political philosopher. she had a background in the [[classics]]. a jewish woman, she survived the holocaust. she studied under and had an affair with [[heidegger]]. she wrote her dissertation on [[augustine]] and [[love]]. she writes about [[marx]]. her influences include [[aristotle]], etc.
