publius ovidius naso (43 BC - 17/18 ad) was a roman poet. his first work was _amores_, three books of love poems. his _magnum opus_ is [[metamorphoses]]. He was exiled by Augustus. He was also clearly influenced by [[virgil]] and [[horace]] and [[callimachus]].
