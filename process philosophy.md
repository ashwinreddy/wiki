> [[heraclitus]] will remain eternally right with his assertion that being is an empty fiction.[^twilight]

process philosophy contrasts ontology in that it focuses on becoming rather than being. Thinkers of process philosophy include [[nietzsche]], [[alfred north whitehead]], and [[deleuze]]. 

## Reading list

* Modes of Thought
* Process and Reality
* Without criteria: Kant, Whitehead, Deleuze


[^twilight]: [[twilight of the idols]] in _the portable nietzsche_, 481.
