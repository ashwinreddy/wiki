_inferno_ is the first canticle of the [[divina commedia]]. 

## canto 1

dante is lost in the woods. he meets [[virgil]]. 

## canto 3

entrance to hell. 

## canto 4

meet [[homer]], [[horace]], [[ovid]], [[lucan]]. 

## canto 5

virgil identifies dido, achilles, and paris.
