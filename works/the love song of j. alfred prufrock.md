_the love song of j. alfred prufrock_ (with unadopted subtitle _prufrock among the women_) is a [[poem]] by [[eliot]] written in 1911, published 1915. it was part of his first book "prufrock and other observations."

the epigraph comes from [[inferno]] 27, lines 61-66. 

[[ezra pound]] was impressed with it.
