_purgatorio_ is the second canticle of the [[divina commedia]]. 

- [[virgil]] and [[dante]] meet [[cato]]
- they find the late-repentant
- they go to the valley of princes
- canto 16: speaking with marco lombardo
- canto 17: virgil discusses love
- canto 18: discussion of free will
- dante and virgil meet statius
- dante meets beatrice
