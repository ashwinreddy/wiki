A **tangent space** $T_pM$ for a [[differentiable manifold]] $M$ is a [[vector space]].

$$
T_p M = \{X_{\gamma , p} | \gamma \text{ smooth curve through } p \}
$$


$$
\dim T_p M = \dim M
$$

Let $\gamma(0)=p$.

\begin{align\*}
X_{\gamma , p}: \mathcal{C}^\infty(M) &\to \RR \\\\\
f &\mapsto (f \circ \gamma)'(0)
\end{align\*}
