For a [[manifold]] $M$, the tangent space $T_pM$ is a copy of $\RR^n$ tangential to $M$ at $p$. A smooth curve $\gamma$ passing through $p$ will have a derivative vector that belongs to $T_p M$.

$$
T_p M = \{\vartheta_{\gamma , p} | \gamma \text{ smooth curve} \}
