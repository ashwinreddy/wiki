The **Kronecker delta** $\delta$ is symbolically written using the [[Iverson bracket]] as

\begin{equation}
\delta_{ij} \triangleq [i = j].
\end{equation}

Similar to [[Dirac delta function]].

# Properties

By symmetry,

$$
\delta_{ij} = \delta_{ji}
$$

$$
\sum_{n = -\infty}^\infty x[n]\delta[n - k] \equiv x[k]
$$
