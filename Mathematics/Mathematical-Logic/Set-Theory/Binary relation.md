A **binary relation** on [[sets|set]] $X$ and $Y$ is a [[subset]] of the [[Cartesian product]] $X \times Y$. In other words, it pairs up some of the $x$ and $y$. If $x$ and $y$ belong to the subset, we denote this $xRy$.
