The **initial value theorem**

# [[Laplace transform]]

If $x(t) = 0$ for all $t<0$ and contains no impulses or singularities at $t=0$, then

$$
x\left(0^+\right) = \lim_{s \to \infty} sX(s)
$$

# [[Z-transform]]

If $x[n]=0$ for $n<0$, then 

\begin{equation}
x[0] = \lim_{z \to \infty} X(z)
\end{equation}
