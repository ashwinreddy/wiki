The **unitary group** $U(n)$ of degree $n$ is the group of $n \times n$ [[unitary matrices|unitary matrix]]. Consider that $U(1)$ is simply a [[complex number]]. $U(1)$ is [[isomorphic|isomorphism]] to $SO(2)$.
