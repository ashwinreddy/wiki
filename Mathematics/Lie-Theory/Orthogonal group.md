The **orthogonal group** $\operatorname{O}(n)$ is the [[Lie group]] consisting of $n \times n$ orthogonal matrices.

# Tangent vectors

Consider a path $A(t)$ going through $I$ at time $t=0$.

\begin{gather}
A(t)A(t)^\mathsf{T} = 1 \\\\\
A'(t)A(t)^\mathsf{T} + A(t)A'(t)^\mathsf{T} = 0 \\\\\
A'(0) + A'(0)^\mathsf{T} = 0 \\\\\
X + X^\mathsf{T} = 0
\end{gather}
