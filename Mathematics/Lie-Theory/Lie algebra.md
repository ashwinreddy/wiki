A **Lie algebra** is a vector space $\mathfrak{g}$ with a binary operation $[,]: \mathfrak{g}\times\mathfrak{g}\to\mathfrak{g}$ that satisfies

Bilinearity
: $[aX+bY, Z] = a[X,Z] + b[Y,Z]$ and $[Z,aX+bY]=a[Z,X]+b[Z,Y]$

Anticommutative
: $[X,Y] = -[Y,X]$

Jacobi identity
: $[X, [Y,Z]] + [Z,[X,Y]] + [Y,[Z,X]]=0$
