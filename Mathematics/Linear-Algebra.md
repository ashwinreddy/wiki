Vectors are invariant. Components are not.

Kronecker delta

$$
\vec{a}\times\vec{b} \equiv \begin{vmatrix}
\mathbf{\hat{i}} & \mathbf{\hat{j}} & \mathbf{\hat{k}} \\\
a_1 & a_2 & a_3 \\\
b_1 & b_2 & b_3
\end{vmatrix}
$$

$$
(\vec{a}\times\vec{b})_i = \sum_{j=1}^{3}\sum_{k=1}^{3}\epsilon_{ijk}a_jb_k
$$
