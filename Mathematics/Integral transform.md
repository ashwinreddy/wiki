An **integral transform** $T$ pushes [[function]] $f$ in one space into a differently parameterized space by integrating over the initial parameter. In terms of an [[integral]],

\begin{equation}
(Tf)(u) = \int_{t_1}^{t_2}f(t) K(t, u) \dd{t}
\end{equation}
