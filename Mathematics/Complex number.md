A **complex number** is a $z = a + b \iota$ for $a, b \in \RR$ and $\iota$ the [[imaginary unit]].

One way to represent a complex number is with a matrix. We can consider this a [[group representation]] for the [[group]] of $(\mathbb{C}, \cdot)$, excluding $z = 0$.

$$
\varphi(a + b\iota) = \begin{bmatrix} a & -b \\ b & a \end{bmatrix}
$$
