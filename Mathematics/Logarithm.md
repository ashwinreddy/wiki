A **logarithm** is the inverse of an [[exponential function]], meaning

\begin{equation}
b^{\log_b a } \equiv a
\end{equation}

\begin{equation}
\log \prod_i x_i = \sum_i \log x_i
\end{equation}

Common bases are 2, $e$, and 10. To write these succinctly, we use $\lg$, $\ln$, and $\log$, respectively.
