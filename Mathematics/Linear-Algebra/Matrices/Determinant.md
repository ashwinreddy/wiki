The **determinant** assigns every [[square matrix]] a scalar with the special property that

\begin{equation}
\det(AB) \equiv \det(A)\det(B)
\end{equation}

# Properties

* $\det I = 1$
* $\det AB = \det A \cdot \det B$
* $\det A^{-1} = \frac{1}{\det A}$
* If $A$ is triangular, $\det A = \prod_i A_{ii}$
