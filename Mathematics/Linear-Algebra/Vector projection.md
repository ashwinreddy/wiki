The **vector projection** of $\vec{a}$ onto $\vec{b}$ finds the closest representation of $\vec{a}$ as $k\vec{b}$.

$$
operatorname{proj} \vec{a} \text{ onto } \vec{b} = { \vec{a}\cdot\vec{b} \over \vec{b}\cdot\vec{b}}\vec{b}
$$
