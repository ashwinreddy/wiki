# Introduction

A vector belongs to the space $\mathbb{R}^n$.

# Operators

## Dot Product

\begin{equation}
\vec{a}\cdot\vec{b} = \sum_i a_ib_i
\end{equation}

## Cross Product

If both vectors belong to $\mathbb{R}^3$,
\begin{equation}
\vec{a}\times\vec{b} = \begin{vmatrix}
\mathbf{\hat{i}} & \hat{j} & \hat{k} \\\
\end{vmatrix}
\end{equation}
