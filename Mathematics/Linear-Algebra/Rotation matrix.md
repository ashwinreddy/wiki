A **rotation matrix** is a matrix $A$ that represents a rotation in the sense that $x \mapsto Ax$ calculates the coordinates of the rotated vector.

# 2D

In 2D, a rotation matrix for angle $\theta$,

$$
R(\theta) = \begin{bmatrix}
\cos \theta & - \sin\theta 
\sin\theta & \cos\theta
\end{bmatrix}
$$
