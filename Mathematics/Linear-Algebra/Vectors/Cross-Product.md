The **cross product** takes two vectors and returns a vector orthogonal to both.

\begin{equation}
\vec{a}\times\vec{b} = \begin{vmatrix}
\mathbf{\hat{i}} & \mathbf{\hat{j}} & \mathbf{\hat{k}} \\\
a_1 & a_2 & a_3 \\\
b_1 & b_2 & b_3
\end{vmatrix}
\end{equation}

The cross product has the following properties
\begin{equation}
\vec{a}\times\vec{b} = |a||b| \sin\theta
\end{equation}

#### Anticommutativity
\begin{equation}
\vec{b}\times\vec{a} = -\left(\vec{a}\times\vec{b}\right)
\end{equation}
