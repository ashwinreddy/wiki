An **inner product** for a [[vector space]] $\mathcal{V}$ is any symmetric, PSD, bilinear  operation $$\langle\cdot , \cdot \rangle: \mathcal{V} \times \mathcal{V} \to \RR.$$ For example, we could define one for [[function]] $f$ and function $g$ as an [[integral]]:

$$
\langle f(x), g(x) \rangle \doteq \int_X f(x) g(x)\\, \dd{x}
$$
