# Miscellaneous Items

The **Cartesian Product** is an operator that returns the combinations of two sets.

\begin{equation}
A\times B = \\{\, (a,b)\, | a \in A \text{ and } b \in B \,\\}
\end{equation}

\begin{equation}
\mathbb{R}^n = \underbrace{\mathbb{R}\times\mathbb{R}\times\dots\times\mathbb{R}}_{n}
\end{equation}
