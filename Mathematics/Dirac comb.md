The **Dirac comb** or **Shah function** is a [[periodic function]] train of [[Dirac delta]] or [[Kronecker delta]] impulses

$$
ле(t) = \sum_{k=-\infty}^\infty \delta(t - k)
$$

If you want the impulses separated by $T$, you use

$$
ле_T(t) = \frac{1}{T}ле\left(\frac{t}{T}\right)
$$

$$
ле[n] = \sum_{k=-\infty}^\infty \delta[n-k]
$$
