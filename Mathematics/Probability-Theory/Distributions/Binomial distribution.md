A **binomial distribution**

$$
X \sim \operatorname{Binomial}(n, p) \iff \mathbb{P}(X = k) = {n \choose k} p^k (1-p)^{n-k}
$$

Of course, [[Bernoulli distribution]] is just the special case of $n=1$.
