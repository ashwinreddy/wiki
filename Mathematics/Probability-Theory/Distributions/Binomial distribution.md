The **binomial distribution** gives the probability of getting $k$ heads out of $n$ flips from a [[Bernoulli distribution]] with probability of heads $p$.

\begin{equation}
f(k, n, p) = \binom{n}{k} p^k (1-p)^{n-k}
\end{equation}
