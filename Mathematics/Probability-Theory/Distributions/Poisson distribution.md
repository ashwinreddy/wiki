A **Poisson distribution** is a [[binomial distribution]] taken to the limit $n \to \infty$.

$$
X \sim \operatorname{Poisson}(\lambda) \iff \mathbb{P}(X = i) = \frac{\lambda^i}{i!}e^{-\lambda}
$$
