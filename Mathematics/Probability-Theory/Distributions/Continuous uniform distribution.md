**Continuous uniform distribution**

\begin{equation}
X \sim \mathsf{Uniform}(a,b) \iff \varphi(x) = \begin{cases}\frac{1}{b-a} & a \leq x \leq b \\\\ 0 & \text{otherwise}\end{cases}
\end{equation}


# Properties

|Property|Value|
|--------|-----|
|[[Expected value]]|$$\frac{a+b}{2}$$|
|[[Variance]]|$$\frac{b-a}{2 \sqrt{ 3}}$$|
