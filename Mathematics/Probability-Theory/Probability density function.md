A **probability density function (PDF/pdf)** is a [[function]] captures the probability of a continuous [[random variable]] with the [[unit interval]].

$$
f : \Omega \to [0, 1]
$$

\begin{equation}
\Pr(a \leq X \leq b) = \int_a^b \varphi(x) \\, \dd{x}
\end{equation}

\begin{equation}

\end{equation}
