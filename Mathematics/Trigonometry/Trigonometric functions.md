The **trigonometric functions** include $\sin$, $\cos$, $\tan$, $\sec$, $\csc$, and $\cot$.

$$
\tan \theta = \frac{\sin \theta}{\cos \theta}
$$

# Trigonometric Identities

$$
\sin^2 \theta + \cos^2\theta = 1
$$

# Exponential form

$$
\sin x = \frac{\iota \left(\exp(-\iota x) - \exp(\iota x) \right)}{2}
$$
