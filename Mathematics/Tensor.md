A $k$-**tensor** is just a linear map

\begin{equation}
T :  V^k \to \RR
\end{equation}

A $k+\ell$-tensor can be formed by the tensor product of a $k$-tensor $T_1$ and an $\ell$-tensor $T_2$.

\begin{equation}
(T_1 \otimes T_2)(v_1, \dots, v_{k + \ell}) = T_1(v_1, \dots, v_k)T_2(v_k, \dots, v_{k + \ell})
\end{equation}
