A **tensor** 


is just a [[multilinear map]] from $k$ copies of a [[vector space]] $V$ to a [[real number]].

\begin{equation}
T :  V^k \to \RR
\end{equation}

Therefore, a [[vector]] and a [[dual vector|dual vector space]] are both tensors.
