The **natural logarithm** is the inverse of the [[exponential function]], the [[logarithm]] with base $e$.

\begin{equation}
\ln \circ \exp = \exp \circ \ln = \text{id}
\end{equation}

A slick way to define it is as an integral.

\begin{equation}
\ln x \triangleq \int_1^x \frac{\dd{u}}{u}
\end{equation}
