A **short exact sequence** is an [[exact sequence]] of the form

\begin{equation}
0\to A\;{\xrightarrow {\ f\ }}\;B\;{\xrightarrow {\ g\ }}\;C\to 0
\end{equation}

\begin{gather}
\operatorname{im}f  = \operatorname{ker}g  \\\\\
\\{f(a) | a \in A\\} = \\{f^{-1}(c) | c\in \operatorname{im}(g) \\}
\end{gather}
