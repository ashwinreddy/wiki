\\[
{\displaystyle \lim _{x\to c}{\frac {f(x)}{g(x)}}=\lim _{x\to c}{\frac {f'(x)}{g'(x)}}}
\\]

**Example**.

\\[
\lim_{x\to 0}\frac{\sin x}{x} = \lim_{x\to 0}\frac{\cos x}{1} = 1
\\]

For this reason, the sine function (for small input) can be approximated up to second order with a Taylor series as:

\\[
\sin x \approx x
\\]
