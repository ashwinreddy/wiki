The **integral** is an operator that gives the area under a curve, at least under the Riemann sum interpretation.

\begin{equation}
\int\limits_a^b{\left[f(x)\right]\,\mathrm{d}g(x)} = \int\limits_a^b{\left[f(x)g'(x)\right]\,\mathrm{d}x}
\end{equation}
