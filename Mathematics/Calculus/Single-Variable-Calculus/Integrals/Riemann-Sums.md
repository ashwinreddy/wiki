Riemann integrals are basically a specification with concrete implementation details of the [integral](../Home)

A formal definition of the **Riemann integral** follows:
\begin{equation}
\int\limits_a^b{f(x)\,\mathrm{d}x} = \lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{b-a}{n}\sum_{i=1}^n{f \left(a+\frac{b-a}{n}i\right)}
\end{equation}

### Average Value

\begin{equation}
\text{average of $f$ on } [a,b] = \frac{1}{b-a}\int\limits_a^b{f(x)\,\mathrm{d}x}
\end{equation}
