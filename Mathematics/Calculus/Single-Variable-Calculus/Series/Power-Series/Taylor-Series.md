\begin{equation}
f(x) = \sum_{n=0}^\infty{\frac{f^{(n)}(a)}{n!}(x-a)^n}
\end{equation}

### Maclaurin Series
\begin{equation}
f(x) = \sum_{n=0}^\infty{\frac{f^{(n)}(0)}{n!}x^n}
\end{equation}

### Examples

\begin{equation}
e^x = \sum_{n=0}^\infty{\frac{x^n}{n!}} \qquad x \in \mathbb{C}
\end{equation}
