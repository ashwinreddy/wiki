
The Gamma function is the continuous extension of [factorials](../../../Discrete Mathematics/Factorial).

# Definition

\begin{equation}
\Gamma(z) \triangleq \int\limits_0^\infty{x^{z-1}e^{-x}\mathrm{d}x}, \qquad z \in \mathbb{R}
\end{equation}

# Factorial Proof

\begin{equation}
\Gamma(n) = (n-1)!
\end{equation}
