The **exponential function** is naturally defined by its [[Maclaurin series]] in Equation \ref{eq:defn}. 


\begin{equation}
\label{eq:defn}
\boxed{
\exp x \triangleq \sum_{k=0}^\infty \frac{x^k}{k!}
}
\end{equation}





\begin{equation}
\exp x = \lim_{n \to \infty} \left(1 + \frac{x}{n}\right)^n
\end{equation}
