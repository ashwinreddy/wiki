There are two parts to the **fundamental theorem of calculus**

\begin{equation}
\dv{}{x}\int_c^x f(x) \,\dd{x} = f(x)
\end{equation}

\begin{equation}
\int_a^b f(x) \,\dd{x} = F(b) - F(a)
\end{equation}

Notice that the second part suggests that

\begin{equation}
\int_a^b \dd{f} = f(b) - f(a)
\end{equation}
