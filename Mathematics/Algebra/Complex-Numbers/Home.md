# Introduction 

A **complex number** is one of the form $a+b\iota$, where $\iota$ is the imaginary number.

## Imaginary Number
The **imaginary number** $\iota$ is the solution to the polynomial equation

$$
x^2 = -1
$$

## Complex Conjugates

\begin{equation}
\bar{z} = \Re{z} - \Im{z}
\end{equation}
