**Soft Actor-Critic** [@haarnoja2018soft] is an off-policy [[actor-critic]] [[maximum entropy reinforcement learning]] approach to optimizing a stochastic [[policy]] $\pi_\phi$ using critic Q-functions $Q_{\phi_1}$ and $Q_{\phi_2}$ and value function $V_\psi$.

It uses the reparameterization trick.
